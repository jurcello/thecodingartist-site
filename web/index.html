The coding artist

What's in the name?

I am Jur de Vries, an independent software developer and musician/artist. Because I am doing both on a professional level, I am a coding artist.

My philosophy

I believe that craftsmanship in any profession starts with a real understanding of the subject. Apart from that I think best results in a profession can be achieved by studying other professions as well. Although not logical at first sight, my education in cello helped me to be a better coder: the discipline to practice every day, the need to try out different interpretations for instance are highly beneficial when coding.

This all might indicate that I advocate a shallow but broad view. That's not the case. I believe that when you do something, you should strive for expert level craftsmanship.
